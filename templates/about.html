Our Mission

Preserving Culture Through Technology

This educational project was undertaken to document, present, and analyze the cultural significance of traditional dances in the Mangaung region.

By leveraging web technologies, we aim to transform academic research into an accessible, engaging, and interactive experience for local communities, historians, and dance enthusiasts alike.

Key Takeaways
  • The deep intersection between historical events and choreography.
  • Challenges and rewards of multimedia archiving.
  • Importance of accessible digital platforms for cultural preservation.
